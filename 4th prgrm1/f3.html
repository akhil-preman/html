MES College of Engineering, Kuttippuram - The Muslim ...MES College of Engineering (MESCE) in Kuttippuram, Kerala, is the state's first self-financing engineering college, founded in 1994 by the Muslim Educational Society (MES). Located along the banks of the Bharathappuzha River, it offers undergraduate (B.Tech, B.Arch), postgraduate (M.Tech, MBA, MCA, M.Arch), and PhD programs. The college is accredited by NAAC with Grade A, approved by AICTE and CoA, and affiliated with the University of Calicut and APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University (KTU).
